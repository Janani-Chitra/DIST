About the Workshop

             Unmanned and autonomous vehicles are widely used in various fields like defence, natural disaster management, public safety, road traffic monitoring, aerial patrol, driverless cars, etc. This workshop aims to share knowledge on unmanned and autonomous vehicles, their applications, developments, etc., to young minds so that they will be motivated to contribute to the field of unmanned and autonomous vehicle developments.

About The Department of Information Science and Technology (DIST)

             The Department functions under the Faculty of Information and Communication Engineering. IST functions as one of the University Departments. It offers Undergraduate, Postgraduate and Research Programmes in various disciplines of Information Technology, Multimedia Technology and Master of Computer Application Degree Programme.


About UAS Laboratory

             The Unmanned & Autonomous Systems (UAS) Laboratory was started in the department of IST in March 2018. The lab is actively engaged in the development of unmanned and autonomous ground robots and quadcopters. We have successfully developed first version of ground robot with collision cone based obstacle detection and avoidance system.The lab is aiming to develop more advanced ground and aerial vehicles for the welfare of the mankind. Currently research scholars, B.Tech (IT), M.C.A. students of IST department are working in this lab under various projects (Realtime and Simulations). This lab offers collaborative projects to passionate students, who want to work in the area of unmanned and autonomous systems.
